Privacy Policy

Introduction

StellarSync is a content operating system for mission-driven organizations. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the service.

The policy applies to the website, onboarding flow, and workspace features that are part of the StellarSync experience.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, organization name, and workspace settings.
  • Content you create, organize, upload, or import into the platform.
  • Usage data, device data, and logs that help us operate and improve the service.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain the service.
  • Support onboarding, billing, setup, and account communications.
  • Improve the product, troubleshoot issues, and prevent abuse.

Data Storage

StellarSync may store content and operational data in services such as Google Sheets, Supabase, and media storage systems used to support the workspace experience.

We store only what is needed to provide the features you enable, and we keep system data separated from customer content where practical.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, payment processing, file storage, and workflow integrations. Those providers handle data according to their own terms and policies.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect the service and customer data. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to reduce risk and limit exposure.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

User Rights

  • Request access to or correction of your information.
  • Request deletion of your workspace data where applicable.
  • Export content or ask questions about how data is used by contacting support.

Children’s Privacy

StellarSync is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always live at /legal/.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to support@stellarsync.app.
