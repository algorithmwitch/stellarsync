Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked celestial questions.

How do I get started?

The easiest way is to click "Create Workspace" at the top of the page. You'll be guided through a 2-step setup process to launch your initial command center and invite your first team members.

Can I book a demo?

Yes! For teams of 10 or more, we offer personalized walkthroughs. Select "Sales & Enterprise" in the contact form above, and one of our strategists will reach out to schedule a deep dive.

What is Managed Setup?

Managed Setup is our "white-glove" implementation service. Our technical team handles workspace configuration, data migration from spreadsheets or other tools, and provides dedicated training for your staff to ensure a seamless transition.

Do you offer nonprofit discounts?

Absolutely. Verified 501(c)(3) and international equivalent organizations are eligible for a 20% discount on all monthly plans. Simply reach out via the "General Support" category with your documentation.

How do I contact enterprise sales?

You can email sales@stellarsync.app directly or use the form on this page and select "Sales & Enterprise". We handle custom contracts, SLAs, and security questionnaires for large organizations.
