Common Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your mission.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your orbit at any time from your workspace settings. Changes are prorated immediately.

Do I need Google Sheets?

No, but it's a powerful way to manage content databases. StellarSync has its own internal planning tools, but we sync beautifully with Sheets for advanced users.

Can I connect Monday.com?

Yes! Starting with our Agency and Managed plans, we offer deep two-way synchronization with Monday.com to bring your team's existing task workflows into our content system.

Can I manage multiple clients?

Yes, our Agency plan is specifically built for this. You can create separate workspace environments for each client while maintaining a central hub for shared assets and reporting.

Can I migrate existing content?

Yes. We provide CSV and API-based import tools. If you choose our Managed plan, our technical team will handle the entire migration process for you.

Do you offer nonprofit pricing?

We certainly do. Verified 501(c)(3) and international equivalent organizations are eligible for a 20% discount on all monthly plans. Contact us to apply.

What happens during managed setup?

Our implementation specialists will meet with your team to understand your workflow, configure your custom integrations, migrate your data, and run a dedicated training session for your staff.
