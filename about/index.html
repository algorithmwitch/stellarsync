Why Constellations?

Looking at the night sky, individual stars tell you very little. They are points of light, distant and disconnected.

Patterns reveal meaning. When we connect those points, we see stories, navigation, and intent.

Campaigns work the same way. A single post may perform well or poorly, but understanding how content connects across platforms, audiences, and objectives tells a much richer story.
